2019 - 2020 Tryout Information

Tryout dates have been approved!

How to Register:

Register or Renew Player Account with USAV, found on OKRVA website. Be sure to PRINT a copy of the forms. There is a USAV annual membership fee paid directly to USAV when registering. The registration/renewal asks for the player's club team. Leave that blank until player has committed to a specific team/club. Make note of the player's USAV Membership Number, as we will need that as evidence of being registered for the upcoming season.


IMPORTANT: A player will NOT be allowed to participate in tryouts without having a current 2019-2020 USAV Registration for the upcoming season.


The Pre-Registration Form now takes you directly to a payment service with our payment processing company, "www.Ground.Work". You can speed up the registration process even faster by paying here. There is a small fee to pay with a debit/credit card, but paying from your checking account is fee-free.


If the registration form does not take you to the payment site, please click HERE to Make Payment

What does it cost to play with Wave?

One of our primary desires is to keep the cost as low as possible so that families can afford to be involved with Club Volleyball!

The cost per player for 2019 - 2020 is just $750! YES, you saw this correctly! This is the SAME price we charged for the last 3 years!


Please NOTE: The cost for this coming year will likely be closer to $950 per player, due to a better practice location. I will only be able to determine the final cost once we have been able to finalize the gym location. IF we stay at Thoreau...the cost will still be in the $750 range.



Here is what's included with this fee:

  • 2 Long Sleeve Sublimated Jerseys
  • 1 Pair of Spandex
  • 1 Backpack
  • 1 Warm Up Top
  • 1 Practice Tee
  • Regular Season Coaching Fees
  • Gym Rental for Two 2-hour Practices Per Week
  • Between 6 and 8 Tournaments plus Oklahoma Regionals in May
    NOTE: Depends on Available Tournaments

What is NOT Included:

  • Optional Warmup Bottoms Uniform Upgrade
  • Travel Costs to and from Tournaments.
  • Coaches Travel Costs to and from Tournaments, including meals while away.
