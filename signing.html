2019-2020 Signing Event Information

This page will provide information regarding the upcoming Signing Night.

2019-2020 Signing Night

Saturday, November 9th at 5:30 pm


Location: Christian Chapel (main sanctuary)
7807 E 76th St, Tulsa, OK 74133


For directions, click here: MAP


At the Signing Night, I will spend several minutes discussing various topics. Once I have welcomed everyone and introduced coaches, I will dismiss head coaches and their players so they can meet together. Then, after I have completed covering topics for parents, I will dismiss all parents to meet their fellow parental teammates. During this time, parents will choose volunteers for various roles. After all roles have been voluteered, parents will then submit paperwork and make their initial payment or full payment, if so desired.


This year, we will try to have a separate fitting time over a few days, in order to reduce the duration of the Signing Night! More will come once those days are set.


Paperwork for Signing Night

Please download these forms. Print them, fill them all in prior to Signing Night.


Wave Volleyball Signing Form

This form contains the Player/Parent Agreement and Chaperone Form. Please read Agreement, and both Parent and Player signs that form. The teams will decide on who will become a Chaperone.


Wave Volleyball Financial Agreement Form

This is the Financial Agreement form that must be completed and turned in at the Signing Night. Circle the PAYMENT option you choose. Payments can be made online, using http://ground.work I will send a separate email for that prior to Signing Night. If you prefer to pay by cash or check, that is fine. Our Payment plan is explained on the form.
Checks should be made out to: Wave Volleyball Club


Wave Volleyball Team Handbook

This is the 2019-2020 Wave Volleyball Handbook that should be reviewed, in order to understand the obligations of both the player/parent as well as the club/coaches. The Handbook may also answer some questions you may have.


Player Medical Release Form - 2019-2020

This is the Medical Release Form that must be completed and turned in at the Signing Night.


Also submit a COPY of player's Birth Certificate. It does NOT need to be a "Legal" copy.


TeamApp

Finally, please install the "TeamApp" to your mobile device. This is primarily how we communicate. You will come to understand this as we move forward. Here are the links: App Store or Google Play



If you do not have access to a smart phone or mobile device, you can simply access the TeamApp website and have all the information available that way as well. Click here for TeamApp Website
