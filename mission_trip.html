Costa Rica Volleyball Mission Trip

Join us on this life-changing trip to explore HOW to use Volleyball to minister to other people!

Trip Details

Dates: June 21 to July 3, 2020


Estimated Cost: $1,600 to $1,800 (Airline cost is the key variable component)


Participants: Players aged 15 or older. We can take a few parents are chaperones, but primarily, the trip must have players. :-)


Location of HPS Lodging: 1 hour from Capital city, San Jose, on a beautiful fern farm.


Trip Highlights:Providing VB clinics to kids, competing matches versus CR teams similar in skill levels (followed by sharing Gospel with them), ministering to kids at an orphanage, ministering to a widow’s / women’s ministry, waterfall rappelling & zip line (additional cost), farmer’s market trip, souvenir market trip, team’s choice of: day trip to a beautiful Starbucks coffee farm OR a day trip to a Costa Rica beach, special dinner with host family.


Meals:ALL primarily meals are included. Snacks are on you.


Lodging:ALL lodging is included. Staying at HPS lodge. Each room has 2 large beds and private bathroom with shower.


Uniforms:Will wear Wave uniforms to keep costs down.


Outreach Opportunities:We will visit an orphanage and love on kids. We’ll purchase groceries for a Widows / Women’s ministry and bless them with bags of food that they hand out. The women then prepare a meal for us and share stories with us. A portion of your trip cost will be donated to one of these ministries to bless them. We will provide some volleyball clinics for less experienced volleyball players, followed by a time of sharing the Gospel.


Payments:In order to secure the best airline ticket price, we will need to get an initial deposit of $600 per person attending. We will have a payment plan available for the remainder.

If you are interseted in going on this trip, fill out this form and email it or give it to Dave. CR Trip Interest Form.

Tentative Itinerary can be downloaded here: Itinerary.
