Wave Volleyball Club Fees and Tulsa Area Club Comparison

Wave Fees include the following:

  • 2 Long-Sleeved Sublimated Jerseys
  • 1 Pair Spandex
  • 1 Practice Tee
  • 1 Warmup Jacket
  • 1 Backpack
  • 6 to 8 Tournament Entries (Depending on Available Tournaments)
  • Gym Rental Fees (2 Hours, Twice per Week)
  • Regular Season Coaching Fees

Wave Fees DO NOT include the following:

  • Optional Warmup Bottoms Uniform Upgrade
  • Your Family Travel Costs to and from Tournaments
  • Coaches Travel Costs to and from Tournaments (Mileage, Tolls, and Meals while away)
